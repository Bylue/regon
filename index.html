The idea

It all started when the owner, Lynaxx, had the idea to make a server where you can fight various bosses, explore large dungeons and much more. When he was finally able to put together a small team, everything was implemented directly. The developer, Kuerbisfloh55, then got very involved with datapacks and we were able to progress much faster.
